What is FRC?

Each year, 1018 participates in the FIRST program that is geared toward high school students, the FIRST Robotics Competition, or FRC for short. With the help of their mentors, students have six weeks to fund, design, build, and program a robot that will complete certain tasks. To put their work to the ultimate test, the team goes on to compete at district, state, and eventually international level competitions.

What is STEM?

As the world continues to innovate and progress, the number of jobs involved in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) continues to rise, but there are not enough people to satisfy the demand for workers, so many positions are left unfilled. In recent years, the principles of STEM has become the focus of many curriculums, clubs, and organizations to inspire and empower the next generation to take on careers involved in STEM and fill the many vacancies.
